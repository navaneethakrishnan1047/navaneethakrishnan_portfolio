Micro Precision Works — Brand Identity Design

Illustrator / October 9t,2025

Micro Precision Works is a modern engineering and machinery brand that stands for innovation, precision, and reliability. This project focuses on creating a strong visual identity that reflects the company’s technical excellence and futuristic vision.
The logo is designed with a clean and powerful structure — the word “MICRO” is seamlessly merged with a gear element, representing the brand’s deep connection to engineering precision and advanced manufacturing. The use of bold typography emphasizes strength and trust, while the red and blue color combination reflects energy, confidence, and industrial sophistication.
This identity system was built to connect with audiences worldwide — from professional engineers to technology innovators — highlighting the company’s global standards and forward-thinking mindset. Every detail of the logo symbolizes motion, performance, and accuracy — the core of what Micro Precision Works delivers.

E-Commerce Mobile App — Modern Shopping Experience

Figma / March 21, 2025

This E-Commerce Mobile App project was designed to deliver a seamless, modern, and visually engaging online shopping experience. The main goal was to build a user-centric interface that makes browsing, selecting, and purchasing products effortless for users around the world.
The design combines minimal aesthetics with intuitive navigation, focusing on clarity, accessibility, and comfort. From personalized product recommendations to smooth checkout transitions, every screen was crafted to enhance usability and elevate the shopping journey.
The interface follows a clean and consistent design system, using modern typography, balanced spacing, and a vibrant color palette that evokes trust and energy. Micro-interactions and motion effects were added to make the user experience more engaging and dynamic.
This project showcases my ability to merge UI/UX design principles with strong visual storytelling — creating a digital product that’s both functional and emotionally appealing.
